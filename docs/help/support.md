# Support

## What to send

Include your account email, bank profile, statement month, session ID, and a short description of what happened.

## For extraction problems

Mention whether OCR was enabled and attach screenshots of the affected rows if possible.

## For access problems

Send the email address you used to sign in and the organization name used in the access request.

## For urgent blockers

Report repeated sign-in failures, upload failures for valid files, review pages that cannot load, or incorrect billing totals.
