# Billing and Limits

## Monthly usage

The home page shows a short monthly usage summary after sign-in. The billing page shows more detail.

## OCR and non-OCR

OCR documents and non-OCR documents are counted separately. OCR usually improves extraction for scans or complex statements.

## Limits

The app can block processing when file, page, rate, or monthly billing limits are reached.

## Billing questions

For a billing query, include your account email, the month, the expected total, and a screenshot of the billing page.
